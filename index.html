Philip Martin

Philip Martin is a Vancouver painter of abstract, and figurative art. He works in acrylics, watercolours, and graphite. He began exhibiting in 1994 with a showing of animation-style portraiture. His contemporary abstractions are based on nature and light. This website is a selection of work showcasing the diversity of Martin’s style and expression.
